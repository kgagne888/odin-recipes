Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

The best grilled cheese ever

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic Salt

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of the bread
  2. Add garlic salt to the bread
  3. Add cheese
  4. Put pan over medium heat
  5. Put bread in pan
  6. Add a splash of water and cover
