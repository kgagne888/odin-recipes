Blended Raspberry & Coconut Chia Pudding

Description

This is a blend of raspberry and chia seeds in pudding form. You top it with greek yogurt and some fruit. Very yummy for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Chia Seeds
  • Dessicated Coconut
  • Milk
  • Frozen Raspberries
  • Protein Powder
  • Thick Greek Yogurt
  • Vanilla
  • Extra fruit, chocolate chips, or coconut for toppings, optional

Steps

  1. Add chia seeds and coconut to each jar
  2. Blend the milk, proteinm powder, and raspberries together until smooth
  3. Pour mixture in with coconut and chia
  4. Stir until combined
  5. Set in fridge
